    When we thought it couldn't get any worse...

    They came, over and over again. From Portugal, Spain, France, Great Britain, The Netherlands, Denmark, Baltic, Hanse Towns, Brandenburg, Sweden, Sardinia, Genoa, U.S.A., Mexico, Argentina, Russia and others. As with arabs, we thought they were of the Mankind, and that they're gods resembled our's. But they didn't recognized us nor God. They made at least 35938 reported voyages.

    The investment that made Europe possible

    You could tell it was Death comming personally. The Trojan Horse changed form but not the intentions. When they discovered our gold was good, our land blessed and our spirits fair, it was clear that they would do it. We counted 9213 distinct vessels, taking our riches, children, woman and men. Europe's first industrial revolution was on the way.

    Black Pearls and Diamonds

    We we're the cargo! Because the are clean, strong, educated and beatiful. Because we have the rhythm to cultivate the land, to build cities, and to enjoy ourselves. Family after family, village after village, city after city, kingdom after kingdom, empire after empire, wrapped up and dispatched worldwide. They fed on us. 10645118 we're embarked and 9180918 made it to the Americas. In the end we we're decimated, and those who survived, here and there, we were bent on our knees.

    About Us

    About Ngutu.org:

    Ngutu.org is an idea with the goal to stablish a strong identity, social and economic for the re-connection of the African diasporas with each other and Mother Africa. We call for global solidarity among Black Africans to assume leadership and entrepeneurship towards the implementation of the great African Commomwealth, centered in Africa and with the diaspora playing an integreated role of embassators for Acquisition and Transfer of Knowledgle and Culture. A World where we all belong.
