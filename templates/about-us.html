About Us

About Ngutu.org:

Ngutu.org is an idea with the goal to stablish a strong identity, social and economic for the re-connection of the African diasporas with each other and Mother Africa. We call for global solidarity among Black Africans to assume leadership and entrepeneurship towards the implementation of the great African Commomwealth, centered in Africa and with the diaspora playing an integreated role of embassators for Acquisition and Transfer of Knowledgle and Culture. A World where we all belong.

About me:
